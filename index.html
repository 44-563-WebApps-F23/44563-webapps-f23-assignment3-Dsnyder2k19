Favorite Vacation Spot: New Orleans, Louisiana

I love the atmosphere of New Orleans. A few of my favorite foods are there such as gumbo, jumbalaya, and bread pudding. The seafood there is super fresh, so I loved trying and fish dishes too. The city was fun to explore and its filled with so much history. I went on a riverboat tour and a cajun river ride, both were equally fun.

3 Activies to do in New Orleans

  1. Either riverboat ride
  2. Visiting the World War II Museum
  3. Riding the trams
