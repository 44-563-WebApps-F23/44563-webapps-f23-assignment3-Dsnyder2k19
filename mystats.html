Favorite Food: Gumbo

My favorite food is gumbo. I first learned about gumbo on my first trip to New Orleans. There are a few variations to the food where you can have chicken, andoulie sausage, and/or shrimp. All of these together enhances the flavor of the dish. The soup or roux, is cooked for around 2-3 hours, letting many different flavors sink it to improve the dish even more.
